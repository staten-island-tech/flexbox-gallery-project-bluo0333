Vegetables

Cucumber

A long, green-skinned fruit with watery flesh, usually eaten raw in salads or pickled.

Broccoli

A variety of cabbage bearing heads of green or purplish flower buds that are eaten as a vegetable.

Taro

A tropical Asian plant of the arum family which has edible starchy corms and edible fleshy leaves.

Lettuce

A plant of the daisy family, with edible leaves that are a usual ingredient of salads.

Tomato

A glossy red, or occasionally yellow, pulpy edible fruit that is eaten as a vegetable or in salad.

Onion

An edible bulb with a pungent taste and smell, composed of several concentric layers.

Cabbage

A plant eaten as a vegetable, having thick green or purple leaves surrounding a head of leaves.

Potato

A starchy plant tuber, cooked and eaten as a vegetable.

Celery

A plant of the parsley family, with closely packed succulent leafstalks that are eaten raw or cooked.
